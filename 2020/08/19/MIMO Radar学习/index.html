Unlike a standard phased-array radar, which transmits scaled versions of a single waveform, a MIMO radar system can transmit via its antennas multiple probing signals that may be chosen quite freely.
普通的相控阵无论传输什么信号，每个阵元上的馈的信号是一致的(权值不同). 而MIMO的每一个发射阵元上的信号则是可以较为自由选择的.不过，这里的probing signal 不太理解是什么.

The advantages of a MIMO radar system with both collocated and widely separated antenna elements.
